The publishers of this app (hereafter referred to as "we") make every effort to ensure the protection of your privacy. We do not store your queries. Information is sent via HTTPS to https://developer.nrel.gov ("Data Provider"), which provides the data behind this service. However, with respect to information sent via this app, neither we nor our affiliates make any warranty, express or implied, or assume any legal liability for the final security and privacy of said information, or represent that its use is completely secure. The Data Provider's handling of your information is subject to their policies, which are linked below.

Beginning with version 2.0, this app allows you to utilize your device's Location Services feature in order to populate it with an approximation of your location. We only collect your location so if you explicitly grant us permission to do so. Such data is collected within your computer and is not shared elsewhere, except in order to execute your query. As with inputted location data, this data is not stored.
